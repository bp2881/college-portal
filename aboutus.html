Vignan Institute of Technology and Science

About Us

Vignan Institute of Technology and Science (VITS) is a pioneering educational institution founded in 1999 by Dr. Lavu Rathaiah. With a firm foundation in excellence, VITS has grown into a leading center for engineering, technology, and management education.

Our focus on research-driven learning, modern infrastructure, and industry-oriented teaching equips students with the skills to succeed in the global job market.

Vision

To establish VITS as a world-class center of learning that fosters excellence in science, technology, and innovation, while nurturing responsible and ethical citizens.

Mission

  • Provide world-class academic programs and research opportunities that foster intellectual curiosity, problem-solving skills, and creativity.
  • Build an innovative, inclusive ecosystem that nurtures entrepreneurship, leadership, and social responsibility.
  • Create industry-academic collaborations to prepare students for the evolving needs of the job market and society.
