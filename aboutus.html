Vignan Institute of Technology and Science

About Us

Vignan Institute of Technology and Science (VITS) is a pioneering educational institution founded in 1999 by Dr. Lavu Rathaiah. With a firm foundation in the principles of excellence, the institute has grown into one of the leading centers for engineering, technology, and management education. VITS offers an advanced learning environment where innovation and creativity meet to shape the future of students.

Our focus on research-driven learning, modern infrastructure, and industry-oriented teaching equips students with skills to succeed in the global job market. With over 2500 students, VITS continues to thrive in preparing the next generation of leaders, innovators, and professionals.

Vision

To establish VITS as a world-class center of learning that fosters excellence in science, technology, and innovation while nurturing responsible and ethical citizens with a strong sense of social awareness and global competitiveness.

Mission

  • To provide world-class academic programs and research opportunities that foster intellectual curiosity, problem-solving skills, and creativity.
  • To build an innovative and inclusive ecosystem that nurtures entrepreneurship, leadership, and social responsibility among students.
  • To create industry-academic collaborations that ensure students are well-prepared for the evolving needs of the job market and society.
