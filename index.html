Course Offered

We expertise the students in many Engineering fields, as follows

Civil Engineering (CE)

The B.Tech in Civil Engineering focuses on designing, building, and maintaining infrastructure such as roads, bridges, buildings, and water supply systems. Students learn to address complex environmental and structural challenges.

Computer Science Engineering (CSE)

The B.Tech in Computer Science Engineering focuses on the design, development, and application of computer systems, including software, hardware, networking, and databases. It prepares students for various roles in software development, data analysis, and systems engineering.

Artificial Intelligence & Data Science (AI & DS)

The B.Tech in AI & Data Science focuses on developing intelligent systems and utilizing advanced data analysis techniques. Students gain expertise in machine learning, big data analytics, neural networks, and statistical modeling to solve real-world problems in diverse industries.

Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (CSE AI&ML)

The B.Tech in CSE with AI & ML focuses on building intelligent systems using machine learning algorithms, computer vision, natural language processing, and deep learning. Students learn how to develop AI applications and integrate machine learning into various industries.

Computer Science Engineering with Data Science (CSE DS)

This specialization in Computer Science Engineering focuses on data analysis, machine learning, and data mining techniques. Students learn how to manage and process big data, apply analytics, and create models to derive insights for decision-making in different fields.

Electrical Engineering (EEE)

The B.Tech in Electrical Engineering covers the study of electrical systems, electronics, electromagnetism, and power generation. Graduates work on designing, developing, and testing electrical equipment, systems, and technologies.

Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)

This field deals with the design and development of electronic systems and communication devices. B.Tech students in ECE gain knowledge in circuit design, signal processing, communication systems, and networking.

Electrical and Instrumentation Engineering (EIE)

B.Tech in Electrical and Instrumentation Engineering is aimed at providing knowledge about electrical systems as well as instrumentation techniques used for measurement, control, and automation in industrial and commercial settings.

Information Technology (IT)

The B.Tech in Information Technology focuses on software development, databases, networking, and information management. Students learn how to create and manage IT systems that help businesses and organizations function effectively.

Mechanical Engineering (MECH)

The B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering focuses on designing, analyzing, and manufacturing mechanical systems and devices. Students learn thermodynamics, materials science, and robotics to solve complex engineering problems in manufacturing and design.
